Questions? Answered

How can my school book a bootcamp?
Reach out through our contact form with your school name, grade levels, and preferred dates. We'll schedule a short call to scope the program and confirm a bootcamp window within two weeks.
What age groups do you target?
Our programs are built for students aged 8 to 17, grouped into three grade bands so activities and difficulty match where each student actually is.
How do you prepare students for Olympiads?
Students work through proof-based problem sets, sit timed mock competitions, and get coached by mentors who've judged or competed at the international level — building toward regional, national, and international rounds.
Do you provide the materials and equipment?
Yes. As a student-led, non-profit initiative, we provide all the specialized software setups, curriculum, and expert mentorship completely free of charge. In return, we just ask host schools to provide the physical classroom space and computer labs. Your school doesn't need to purchase any external kits or source specialized materials—we bring the skills and trainers directly to you.
